Visit Pleven

Enjoy culture and history in this vibrant city.

Top three activities to do in Pleven

Visit Skobelev Park

Enjoy a park with fake cannon guns right in the city center.

Visit The Panorama

Visit a place where they recreated battles that have been fought 100 years ago for the freedom and reclamation of Pleven.

Visit Park Kailuka

Go for a picnic in the nature with a long park and consider visiting the Zoo if you like animals.
